##  Project Overview

The Cruise Management and Obstacle Detection System is a smart vehicle assistance system designed to improve driving safety and comfort.

This system uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in front of the vehicle and automatically adjusts speed to prevent collisions. It helps maintain a steady speed and reduces driver effort during long drives.

The project demonstrates how embedded systems and sensors can be applied in modern automotive safety solutions.

---

##  Objectives

- Maintain constant vehicle speed  
- Detect obstacles in front of the vehicle  
- Automatically reduce speed when obstacles are close  
- Improve fuel efficiency  
- Reduce driver workload  
- Enhance driving safety
